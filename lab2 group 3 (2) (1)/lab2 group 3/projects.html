Project Overview

EcoTrack is a comprehensive sustainability platform designed to help businesses track, analyze, and reduce their environmental impact. The platform provides actionable insights through intuitive dashboards, automated reporting, and customizable goals.

Key Features

  • Real-time carbon footprint tracking
  • Customizable sustainability dashboards
  • Automated reporting and compliance tools
  • Benchmark comparison with industry standards
  • Actionable recommendations for improvement
